# Honours Deliverables

## Overview
This repository contains my Honours thesis and seminar presentation on leveraging large language models (LLMs) to convert unstructured clinical text into structured knowledge graphs. The work focuses on evaluating whether these representations improve ICD-10-CM principal diagnosis prediction compared to baseline plaintext methods.

The research compares multiple knowledge graph construction approaches and models, analysing their impact across different levels of classification accuracy. The included materials summarise the full methodology, experiments, and key findings.

## Files

### Thesis
Final submitted Honours thesis presenting the full research, including background, methodology, experimental evaluation, and conclusions.

### Seminar Presentation
Accompanying seminar presentation slides providing a concise overview of the research, key methods, and main findings.
